    • Power on One Penny

    The power of one penny is monumental over time. Every dollar was once a penny just as every invention was once dream.

    We've launched a global initiatives to encourage every single person to value the small things in life, from a smile to a penny.
    This initiative was started in an effort for people to recognize each progressive step in the quest to achieve a wonderful legacy.
    Each person is cheered on to save all of their pennies for an entire year. Then, this change will be used to make change.
    These transformations can be in social causes and collegiate funds. We are convinced that there is untapped power that
    outpours when each dollar is given a goal. Hence, we must start with the smallest mass of money, the brown penny.
    What is your savings goal for year? How will you add value to your last name? Your community? Your dreams?.




































    Empowerment Tour & Events

    The power equation of economics and education10,000,000 global, generational mission and the rise from very beginning.

    The global empowerment tour launched in February 2016 for Gianna and the esteemed squad. It was created to give people the courage to reclaim the life they envisioned as a fearless, cheerful child.
    An untainted canvas of goals can be restored as people successfully complete the programs. The tours focus on self-worth, education, economics and power. Colleges, corporations, churches and all organizations are welcome to inquire about bringing the tour to their city. Since embarking on this tour, the team has impacted Columbus, Atlanta, Birmingham, Macon, Charleston, Columbia, Dallas, New Orleans, Philadelphia and this is just the genesis.

    As a grand kickoff to 2016, there will be an activating, dream-building workshop. The Super Dream Bowl will take place this November 9th, 2016 in Atlanta, GA. This vision session will be spearheaded by the Money Cheerleader and the team of professionals, athletes, and more successful make-it-happen trailblazers. This hands-on workshop is designed to give participants strategies to grow in the eight areas of life, starting with self. By the end, every participant will have a game plan to win big.
